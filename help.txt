Available Commands:

1. state
Description: Categorizes data by US state
Options:
'price' -- Orders data by average max. price/person of each states top 10 restaurants
'rating' -- Orders data by average star rating of each states top 10 restaurants

2. type
Description: Categorizes data by restaurant type
Options:
'price' -- Orders data by average max. price/person of all types of restaurants in the US
'rating' -- Orders data by average star rating of all types of restaurants in the US

3. exit
Description: Terminates the program
